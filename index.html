About Me

I am a highly motivated and results-oriented IT professional with over 18 years of experience in various roles, including Pre-Sales Solution Development, IT Mentoring, Data Analytics Instruction, Project & Service Management, and Systems Engineering.

My expertise lies in NetSuite ERP, where I excel in customizing and configuring solutions, managing integrations (NetSuite-SFDC, NetSuite-OpenAir, NetSuite-Shopify), and providing technical support. I am also proficient in Data Analytics and possess strong instructional and mentoring skills, having guided students in various IT and Data Analytics programs.

Throughout my career, I have consistently demonstrated a strong ability to:

  • Solve complex problems: I thrive in challenging situations and find innovative solutions to overcome obstacles.
  • Collaborate effectively: I am a strong team player with excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Learn and adapt: I am a quick learner and eager to acquire new skills and technologies to stay ahead of industry trends.
  • Deliver exceptional results: I am dedicated to exceeding expectations and achieving project goals.

I am passionate about technology and its potential to transform businesses and improve lives. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow and contribute to the success of the organizations I work with.
